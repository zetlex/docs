<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Service Management">
    To find the login data for your service, please go to https://panel.bytehosting.cloud. Once there, locate the service list and click **"Manage"** next to the service you want to access.
    ![title](/images/manage.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Login Data">
    Then, click on the **"Show Login Data"** button.
    ![title](/images/logindata.png)

    That's it!
    
    <Tip>You can find detailed guides for connecting to your service here.  
    [Setting Up Your Server with Windows Server](/general-information/windows)  
    [Connecting to Your Linux Server](/general-information/linux)</Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
