title: 'Renewing Your Service'
description: 'A quick guide renewing your service on our control panel.'
icon: "dollar"
iconType: "solid"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Service Management">
    To find the login data for your service, please go to https://panel.bytehosting.cloud. Once there, locate the service list and click **"Manage"** next to the service you want to renew.
    ![title](/images/manage.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Extend">
    Then, click on the **"Extend"** button.
    ![title](/images/logindata.png)

    Choose between **PayPal**, **Stripe** and **Cryptocurrency**.  
    Select the amount of days you want to add to the service.  
      
    That's it!
  </Step>
</Steps>
